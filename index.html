Meet DreamBars

DreamBars are delicious, functional chocolates formulated with three key compounds that gently guide you into deep, restorative sleep.

L-Theanine

A naturally occurring amino acid found in tea leaves. It promotes relaxation without drowsiness, reduces anxiety, and enhances alpha brain waves associated with calm alertness.

Magnesium

Magnesium supports muscle relaxation, reduces stress, and helps regulate melatonin—the hormone that guides your sleep-wake cycle. Essential for deep, uninterrupted rest.

5-HTP

5-Hydroxytryptophan is a compound your body uses to produce serotonin. It helps elevate mood and promotes natural melatonin production for smoother sleep transitions.

DreamBars are melatonin-free — crafted for restful sleep without hormones or groggy wakeups.
